Coding Challenge

Theme: Turn-based Combat

  • Take turns attacking
  • Ability to choose between different attacks
  • Add health system for player and enemy
  • Ability to use items during combat
  • Add animations for various actions (e.g. attacking, using items, etc.)
  • Add chance to miss and/or do critical damage
  • Add messages that narrate the actions being taken by the player and enemies
  • Gain experience points to level up
  • Ability to level up stats or unlock new skills
  • Add exploration to encounter new enemies
  • Plus... any other cool ideas you come up with! Be creative!
